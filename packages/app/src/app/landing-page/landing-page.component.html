ReefGuide

A decision support platform for reef intervention planning and analysis

ReefGuide integrates multiple data layers and analytical capabilities to help reef managers and researchers make informed decisions about reef restoration and adaptation interventions across the Great Barrier Reef.

ReefGuide Capabilities

filter_list

Reef Criteria Assessment

Filter suitable reefs based on customisable ecological and bathymetric criteria across the entire Great Barrier Reef.

location_on

Site Placement Optimisation

Identify optimised deployment locations within viable reefs using configurable deployment dimensions.

analytics

ADRIA Analysis

Perform scenario analysis at site scale (Moore Reef cluster) and reef scale (whole GBR) using adaptive reef intervention algorithms.

Your Projects

Projects in ReefGuide organise your analysis work. Each project contains your specific configurations, results, and visualizations for either Site Assessment or ADRIA Analysis workflows.
